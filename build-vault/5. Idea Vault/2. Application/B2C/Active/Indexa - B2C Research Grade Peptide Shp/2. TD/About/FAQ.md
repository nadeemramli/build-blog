WHAT ARE PEPTIDES AND HOW DO THEY WORK?

Peptides are naturally occurring amino acid chains that act as messengers in the body, supporting specific functions like energy production, recovery, metabolism, and anti-aging. They work by signaling your body to enhance its natural processes, optimizing your overall health and performance.

## DO I NEED TO REFRIGERATE MY PEPTIDE?

If your peptide is still in powder form, it can be stored at room temperature. However, once mixed with bacteriostatic (BAC) water, it must be refrigerated to maintain stability and effectiveness. NAD and Cerebrolysin are delivered ready-for-use in an insulated sleeve and do not require reconstitution. These peptides should be refrigerated immediately upon receipt.

## WHERE SHOULD I INJECT MY PEPTIDE?

Inject subcutaneously into the stomach, about 3 inches to the left or right of the belly button. Do not inject into a vein, use in an IV, or consume injectable peptides orally.

## HOW DO I RECONSTITUTE MY PETPIDE?

To mix your peptide, inject all the bacteriostatic water from the syringe into the peptide vial. Let it fully dissolve before use. If you need further guidance, refer to the instructions provided with your order.
