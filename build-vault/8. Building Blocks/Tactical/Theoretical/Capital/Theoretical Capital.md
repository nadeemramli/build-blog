# Theoretical Capital

> [!summary]
> Capital tactics we've **observed or collected** but not yet built — ideas for raising, allocating, or wielding cash, plus finance/accounting mechanics worth trying. Untrusted until tested. Governed by [[Capital]]; lives under [[Theoretical]].

## Scope
Idea candidates across Capital's work:
- **Raise** — funding instruments or routes seen elsewhere.
- **Allocate** — reinvestment frameworks and capital-allocation heuristics.
- **Wield** — ways to deploy cash offensively in [[Market]] or [[Operation]].
- **Machinery** — bookkeeping/accounting/finance tools or workflows to evaluate.

## How to write a note here
Capture fast and loose: *the observation (with source link) · the hypothesis · rough cost to test*.

> [!caution] Candidates, not recommendations
> Nothing here is proven. Promote to [[Practical Capital]] only after building and executing — and remember money-moving stays human-gated ([[Capital#Automation notes]]).
