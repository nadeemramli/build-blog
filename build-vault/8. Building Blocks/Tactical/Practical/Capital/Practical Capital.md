# Practical Capital

> [!summary]
> Capital tactics we've **built and executed** — concrete moves to raise, allocate, or wield cash, plus the bookkeeping/accounting/finance mechanics we've actually put in place. Governed by [[Capital]]; lives under [[Practical]].

## Scope
Proven tactics across Capital's work:
- **Raise** — funding routes we've used (retained earnings, debt, equity).
- **Allocate** — real reinvest-vs-hold calls we've made and their outcomes.
- **Wield** — times we deployed cash offensively in [[Market]] or [[Operation]].
- **Machinery** — bookkeeping, accounting, and finance workflows we run.

## How to write a note here
Each note: *what we did · the result (with numbers) · keep / kill / iterate*. Pair with the [[decision-log.template|decision-log]] entry.

> [!caution] Money-moving stays human-gated
> Even documented Capital tactics keep a human approval gate on anything that moves money or files with authorities — see [[Capital#Automation notes]].
