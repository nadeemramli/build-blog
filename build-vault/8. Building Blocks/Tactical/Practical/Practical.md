# Practical

> [!summary]
> Tactics we have **built and executed**. This is the trusted shelf of the [[Tactical]] layer — every note here is a record of something that actually ran, what it cost, and what it returned. Documentation, not speculation.

## What belongs here

A tactic enters Practical only after it has been *implemented and run at least once*. The note then captures:

- **What we did** — the concrete play, setup, and assets.
- **The result** — what happened, with numbers where possible.
- **The learning** — keep / kill / iterate, and why.

If a note can't answer "what happened when we ran it?", it isn't Practical yet — it belongs in [[Theoretical]].

## The three domains

| Domain | Tactics about | Governed by |
|---|---|---|
| **[[Practical Market]]** | Branding, product, distribution/growth moves we've shipped | [[Market]] |
| **[[Practical Capital]]** | Raising, allocating, wielding cash — executed | [[Capital]] |
| **[[Practical Operation]]** | Costing, supply chain, inventory, fulfilment — executed | [[Operation]] |

> [!tip] Pair every Practical note with a [[decision-log.template|decision-log]] entry
> The decision log is the dated proof of execution. A Practical note is the distilled, reusable playbook that the log earns.
