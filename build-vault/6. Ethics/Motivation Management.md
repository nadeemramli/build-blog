It's easy to look forward a bit more than needed for much better planning, this causes us to remove the reward system produced by the journey.

Now the journey seems a bit "boring" due to the fact, that we're seeing the outcome of it, predicted the outcome of it, hence the "Reward" diminished, making us less motivated. 

A remedy for this is as easy as this:

1. Get into the flow (if learning can't get you to the flow, then building will)
2. See how far you've come, instead of how far left you need to walk. (Reflect)
