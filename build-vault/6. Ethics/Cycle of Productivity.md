Understanding Cycle of Productivity

High in Energy - (Doing/Executing/Troubleshoot)  
  - Working (Actually doing the work, finishing task)  
  - Active Lifestyle  
High in Knowledge - (Consolidating/Reflection/Documentation)  
  - Personal Knowledge Management  
  - Reading / Exploring / Learning  
  - Writing / Building Models  
High in Direction - (Planning/Strategy)  
  - Writing / Planning  
  - Ideas / Road mapping / Prioritization  
High in Serendipity - (Resting/Acceptance)  
  - Meditation / Breathing

The keyword here is agency, cause agency is hard, it's by nature is hard (if a business hard, agency 10X harder), the idea lies solely with the fact that anyone can start an agency, but it's hard for us to see sustainable, successful and big agency (due to the nature of agency which relies on survival of the fittest model). So if you learn and understand how big agency operate, there's a lot for us to learn there, to understand productivity.

Micro-learning are the one we do on daily basis to proceed and keep on moving forward, what people usually meant by high energy is this. Do it, failed, then keep on doing, then failed, but then proceed on doing it.
