## Takeaways
1. **Affiliate program in main nav** — they outsource distribution to commission resellers. Mirrors our dropship tier ([[Pricing Model]]); consider productising it as a public "partner" page instead of private arrangements.
2. **GLP-aliasing** ("GLP-3R", "GLP-2T") in catalog naming = platform-safe SKUs end-to-end, not just in content. Aligns with our restricted-terms gate ([[indexa-zernio-publishing]]).
3. **Vial case holder ($8)** — tiny accessory raises AOV; trivial for us to add.
4. Their per-mg Reta at 50mg (~RM15.7/mg) is the realistic price ceiling check for our RM1,000/60mg — we're competitive, no repricing pressure.
5. Sloppy ops (price mismatches, mass OOS flags) — reliability + stock honesty is an easy trust win for us.
