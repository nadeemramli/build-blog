## Takeaways
1. **Strongest strategic comp of the four.** Format multiplication (same compound as isolate/caps/nasal/topical/high-dose) turns ~40 compounds into 126 SKUs and price-discriminates without new molecules. Our R&D stock could seed 1–2 format variants (e.g. topical GHK).
2. **Their alias system is our Threads rule applied to the storefront** ([[indexa-content-threads-strategy]]) — naming, slugs ("r3ta"), and brand all platform-safe. Proof the approach scales to a full business.
3. **Wolverine at RM359 vs our RM350** — our blend price is market-correct globally, not just vs Olymp.
4. Login-wall + EIN/business fields = lead capture w/ compliance theatre; referral program + merch builds identity ("Alpha Researcher"). Community/identity is a moat we haven't started.
