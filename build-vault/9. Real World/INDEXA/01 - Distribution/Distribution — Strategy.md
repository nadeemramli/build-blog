# 🟣 Distribution

**Mandate:** generate qualified demand and convert it into paid orders — profitably and on-brand. Distribution feeds Operation (orders to fulfil) and Capital (revenue to bank).

## Objective & guardrail

Grow revenue per month while holding blended **ROAS ≥ 2.0** and keeping every message in **research-use** framing (no dosing/medical claims — this is non-negotiable and is what keeps the brand alive).

## Funnel

```
Awareness  → Interest → Consideration → Purchase → Retention
(content)    (lead)      (nurture)       (order)    (membership/repeat)
```

| Stage | What we do | Logged as |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Awareness | TikTok/IG/Threads/X content, SEO blog, COA transparency | `#content-post` |
| Interest | DM/WhatsApp enquiry, profile/link click | `#lead` |
| Consideration | Answer questions, send guides, offer bundle fit | `#lead` (status `nurturing`) |
| Purchase | Website checkout or WhatsApp-assisted order | `#sales-order` |
| Retention | Membership, reorder nudges, newsletter | `#campaign` (retention) |

## Channels (current)

Instagram · Threads · X · Telegram (@indexalabs) · TikTok · WhatsApp enquiry · Email (newsletter). TikTok is the top-of-funnel engine; WhatsApp is the close. Website is the system of record for orders.

## Offer architecture

- **On-demand singles** — ~30 peptides (BPC-157, Retatrutide, MOTS-C, GHK-Cu, PT-141, Epithalon …).
- **Curated bundles** — the conversion drivers, grouped by goal: *To Feel Good, Shred, Lean & Mean, Glow Up, Deep Sleep, Longevity Stack, Wolverine Protocol* and more.
- **Promotions:** Free shipping > RM200 · Free shipping + 10% > RM500.
- **Trust assets:** third-party COA / test reports, product Verify, order tracking. These *are* the marketing — research buyers convert on proof.

## Brand benchmarks

These are **not competitors** — they're the brands whose *look, voice, and distribution machinery* we want to grow into. Most are licensed-clinician telehealth (a different regulatory model from ours), so we copy their **brand and distribution mechanics, never their medical/dosing claims**. Our guardrail is unchanged: research-use framing, COA/Verify as the proof engine. AgelessRx and Voy are the north stars; the rest are the supporting roster, each picked for one transferable move.

> How to read this: ignore their drug claims, study how they earn trust, package an offer, and move a stranger to a paying, repeating customer.

### North stars

**[AgelessRx](https://agelessrx.com)** — US longevity telehealth.
*Positioning:* not general telehealth — specifically "slow/reverse aging," healthspan-led, every protocol reviewed by a provider.
*Why strong:* authority is the product. A Stanford-PhD research team runs its own trials; "monitoring + bloodwork included" reframes price as safety, not cost. 30+ protocols sold as goal-based stacks.
*Distribution:* subscription + automatic refills = built-in retention; protocol catalog + "longevity journey" framing drives reorders.
*Steal for Indexa:* lean into our **Research / Literature pillar** as the brand's spine — cite the science, show the COA, let proof do the selling. Package singles into **goal-based stacks** (we already do: Longevity Stack, Wolverine) and frame every label as "verified / third-party tested," our version of "monitoring included."

**[Voy](https://www.joinvoy.com)** — UK GLP-1 / metabolic telehealth (Manual's weight-loss sub-brand; 100k+ members in 18 months).
*Positioning:* calm, anti-hype "proactive companion," explicitly *not* a "pill-mill." Clinical but warm.
*Why strong:* a companion **app + human coach + AI assistant (Joy)** turns a transactional refill into an ongoing relationship; a results guarantee ("10% in 6 months or money back") sells confidence.
*Distribution:* subscription with coaching baked in; consult → clinician review → ship → ongoing app engagement = high retention.
*Steal for Indexa:* the **muted, clinical-but-human visual language** is exactly our packaging/labelling upgrade target. Our "companion" is cheaper to build: a **WhatsApp/Telegram close + reorder nudges + reconstitution/storage guides** that make a buyer feel supported, not just sold to.

### Supporting roster

**[Function Health](https://www.functionhealth.com)** — membership lab testing (~$365/yr, 160+ biomarkers).
*Steal:* data/proof *is* the product. Mirrors our COA/Verify edge — position transparency as a paid, recurring value, not a giveaway. Membership = predictable revenue.

**[Superpower](https://superpower.com)** — viral biomarker testing; grew via organic content, not ads.
*Steal:* the most relevant to our **TikTok + Threads** paths. Shareable proof artifacts (their "Bio Age cards," a hoodie drop) = build *our* shareable COA-reveal / "verify your vial" content and let users spread it. Growth without ad spend protects ROAS.

**[Eden](https://www.tryeden.com)** — GLP-1 DTC with flat, transparent pricing (no price hikes as dose climbs).
*Steal:* **radical pricing transparency** as a trust weapon — the pricing analogue of our COA transparency. Publish clear prices and free-shipping thresholds; never make the buyer feel a hidden markup.

**[Novos](https://novoslabs.com)** — longevity supplements "designed by aging researchers," accessible price, explicitly counter-positioned against Bryan Johnson's $2M Blueprint.
*Steal:* the **"serious science, accessible price"** lane — exactly where we sit vs premium sellers like Olymp. Authority *without* elitism; we undercut on price while out-proving on COA.

**[Maximus Tribe](https://www.maximustribe.com)** — performance/men's telehealth; tiered protocol packs (microdose → starter → custom).
*Steal:* **tiered protocol packaging** validates our bundle architecture. Offer clear entry → standard → advanced tiers so an agent can upsell along a ladder instead of selling one SKU.

**[Numan](https://www.numan.com)** — UK men's health; building "a consumer brand around healthcare," destigmatising, expanding into a "complete health partner."
*Steal:* **brand-first, category-expansion** thinking — one consistent identity across every channel and agent (our Brand-improvement pipeline), then widen from peptides into adjacent goals once trust is banked.
