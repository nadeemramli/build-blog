# SOP — Content Publishing

**Goal:** keep a steady, on-brand content pipeline that feeds the top of funnel.

**Cadence:** plan weekly, batch-produce, schedule 7 days ahead.

## Steps
1. **Pick the angle** from a content pillar (education / product / social-proof / promo). Keep a 2:1:1 ratio of education-or-proof to product to promo.
2. **Create the note** — copy `_Template — Content Post`, name it `POST-YYYYMMDD-NNN`, set `channel`, `format`, `pillar`, `publish_date`, `hook`.
3. **Write to the guardrail** — research-use framing only. No human dosing, no "treats/cures", no before/after medical claims. COA and process content is encouraged.
4. **Status flow:** `idea → drafting → scheduled → published`.
5. **Publish**, then paste the live `link` back into the note and set `published`.
6. **Hand off demand** — when a post drives DMs, create `#lead` notes (see [[SOP — Lead to Sale]]).

## Definition of done
Post is `published`, `link` filled, and any inbound interest captured as leads.

## China-comment note
On TikTok, block/limit off-topic spam comments per the build journal policy before they pile up.
