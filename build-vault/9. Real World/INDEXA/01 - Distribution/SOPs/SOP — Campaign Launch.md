# SOP — Campaign Launch

**Goal:** launch a paid or promo campaign with a clear objective and a measurable ROAS.

## Steps
1. **Define objective:** `awareness`, `traffic`, `conversion`, or `retention`.
2. **Create the note** — copy `_Template — Campaign`, name `CMP-NNN`, set `channel`, `objective`, `start`, `end`, `budget`.
3. **Set the offer** — which bundle/SKU, which promo (e.g. free shipping > RM200), and the landing destination.
4. **Go live** → status `live`. Log `spend` and `revenue` at least weekly.
5. **Read ROAS** (Base computes `revenue / spend`):
   - ROAS ≥ 2.0 → consider scaling budget.
   - 1.5–2.0 → optimise creative/offer.
   - < 1.5 after a fair test → `paused`, document why.
6. **Close** → status `done`, write a 3-line retro in the note body.

## Escalate
Any budget increase or new paid channel → confirm with Nadeem first.
