# Glycine

## Overview

## Role in stacks
- `[anecdote]` Pairs with [[Pinealon]] (injectable) for sleep; oral 3–5 g, double to 10 g if no effect. — [[TRANSCRIPT-huberman-koniver-peptides|Koniver]] 01:30–01:35

## Dosing & intake
- **Dose:** 3–5 g, up to 10 g at bedtime (oral); also injectable
- **Route:** oral or injectable
- **Timing:** bedtime
- **Intake:** any

## Cautions
- Not sedating — tones down the nervous system; also supports **phase-2 liver detox** (amino-acid conjugation).
