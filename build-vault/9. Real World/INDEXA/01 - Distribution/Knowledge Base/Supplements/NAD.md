# NAD

## Overview

## Role in stacks
- `[anecdote]` Drives electron flow at **complex I** (the most common 'traffic jam'); Koniver's single most impactful agent — rapid mood/energy/cognition lift after a loading dose. — [[TRANSCRIPT-huberman-koniver-peptides|Koniver]] 01:52–02:04

## Dosing & intake
- **Dose:** IV: 750 mg ×5 over 10 days (loading), then monthly. SubQ: 100 mg, 5 on / 2 off.
- **Route:** IV or SubQ (not absorbed orally)
- **Timing:** any (IV/SubQ infusion)
- **Intake:** n/a

## Cautions
- Infusion discomfort if dripped too fast (chest tightness, leg pressure). Precursors: [[Nicotinamide Riboside]], [[NMN]].
