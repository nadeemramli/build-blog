# Magnesium L-Threonate

## Overview
## Role in stacks
- A magnesium form that crosses into the CNS; used in sleep stacks for relaxation/sleep quality.
## Dosing & intake
- **Dose:** ~1.5–2 g (≈144 mg elemental Mg) · **Route:** oral · **Timing:** bedtime · **Intake:** any

## Cautions
- GI upset at high doses; the threonate form is chosen for brain penetration.
