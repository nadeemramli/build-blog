# Methylene Blue

## Overview

## Role in stacks

- `[anecdote]` Electron donor at **complex IV** (cytochrome-c oxidase); cognitive stimulant, mild MAOI, appears antiviral. — [[TRANSCRIPT-huberman-koniver-peptides|Koniver]] 01:44–01:50
- `[note]` Prescription drug also sold OTC as a supplement — sourcing/purity caution applies.

- `[anecdote-bb]` Despite being a phenothiazine *derivative*, **no evidence it lowers GH secretion** — safe to use for mitochondrial/cognitive purposes alongside a GH-secretagogue protocol. — [[TRANSCRIPT-vigorous-steve-gh-secretagogues|Vigorous Steve]]

## Dosing & intake
- **Dose:** 10 mg (oral); up to 50 mg IV in-clinic
- **Route:** oral (also IV)
- **Timing:** morning
- **Intake:** with or without food

## Cautions
- Mild **MAOI** — avoid with SSRIs/SNRIs/other serotonergics. Turns urine blue-green; absence of colour may indicate poor mitochondrial function.
