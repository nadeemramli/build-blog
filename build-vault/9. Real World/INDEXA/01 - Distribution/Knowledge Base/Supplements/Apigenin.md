# Apigenin

## Overview
## Role in stacks
- A chamomile-derived flavonoid used as a calming/sleep aid (binds benzodiazepine sites); common in sleep stacks with [[Glycine]] and magnesium.
## Dosing & intake
- **Dose:** ~50 mg · **Route:** oral · **Timing:** bedtime · **Intake:** any

## Cautions
- Generally well tolerated; mild sedation.
