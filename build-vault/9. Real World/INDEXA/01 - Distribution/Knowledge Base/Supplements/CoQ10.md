# CoQ10

## Overview

## Role in stacks
- `[anecdote]` Electron carrier at **complex III** (Koniver's cytochrome-3); dramatic migraine reduction reported. — [[TRANSCRIPT-huberman-koniver-peptides|Koniver]] 01:01–01:05

## Dosing & intake
- **Dose:** 200 mg/day (studied safe up to 2,400 mg)
- **Route:** oral
- **Timing:** morning
- **Intake:** **with a fat-containing meal** (fat-soluble — improves absorption)

## Cautions
- Generally well tolerated.
