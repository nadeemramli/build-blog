# MK-677 (Ibutamoren)

## Overview
- `[educational]` A **non-peptide, orally-bioavailable** small molecule that activates the same growth-hormone-secretagogue receptor (GHSR-1A) as the GHRPs. Note: **not a peptide** — modeled here as a `kb-compound`. — [[TRANSCRIPT-gh-peptide-selection-deep-dive|GH selection guide]]

## Mechanism & effects
- `[educational]` **Most human data of any GHRP-class agent** (incl. a 500+ patient Alzheimer's year-long trial); +70% IGF-1 in older adults. Long half-life → raises **trough GH ~3.5×** (non-physiologic, like CJC-DAC) plus pulse amplitude. Oral 20–30 mg nightly. — [[TRANSCRIPT-gh-peptide-selection-deep-dive|GH selection guide]]

## Cautions
- `[warning]` **Insulin resistance** (+5 glucose, +0.2 A1C over 2 yr), **appetite** stimulation, mild ACTH/cortisol/prolactin; one trial stopped for **CHF risk**. Less physiologic than [[Ipamorelin]]. In FDA stage-3 pipeline.
