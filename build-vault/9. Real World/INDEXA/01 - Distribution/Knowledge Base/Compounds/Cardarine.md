# Cardarine (GW501516)

## Overview
- `[educational]` A **non-peptide PPARδ agonist** that boosts endurance/cardio capacity by shifting metabolism. Originally a diabetes candidate. — [[TRANSCRIPT-anabolic-roundtable-peptides|Anabolic Roundtable]]

## Cautions
- `[warning]` **Scrapped in development after an animal cancer signal**; WADA-banned. Included here as a cautionary reference, not a recommendation.
