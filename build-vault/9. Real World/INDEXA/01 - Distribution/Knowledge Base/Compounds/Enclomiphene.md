# Enclomiphene

## Overview
- `[educational]` A **SERM** — the active *trans*-isomer of clomiphene (without zuclomiphene's estrogenic, long-half-life baggage). **Non-peptide**; an Indexa catalog product. — [[TRANSCRIPT-trt-testosterone-optimization|TRT/testosterone guide]]

## Mechanism & effects
- `[educational]` Blocks hypothalamic estrogen receptors → ↑GnRH → ↑LH/FSH → ↑endogenous testosterone (preserves fertility, unlike TRT). ~12.5–25 mg. Not FDA-approved (cleaner alternative to [[Clomiphene]]). — [[TRANSCRIPT-trt-testosterone-optimization|TRT/testosterone guide]]
