# Clomiphene (Clomid)

## Overview
- `[educational]` A **SERM** (mix of enclomiphene + zuclomiphene); FDA-approved for female infertility, used off-label to raise male testosterone. **Non-peptide.** — [[TRANSCRIPT-trt-testosterone-optimization|TRT/testosterone guide]]

## Mechanism & effects
- `[educational]` Blocks hypothalamic estrogen receptors → ↑LH **and FSH** → ↑testosterone **and sperm** (good for fertility). 25 mg EOD → 50 mg; best for secondary hypogonadism, age <45. Can raise estrogen (zuclomiphene); blunted by high prolactin. — [[TRANSCRIPT-trt-testosterone-optimization|TRT/testosterone guide]]
