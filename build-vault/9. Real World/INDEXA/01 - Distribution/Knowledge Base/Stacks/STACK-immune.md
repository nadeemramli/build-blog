# Immune / Resilience Protocol

> [!info] Goal
> Counter **[[Immunosenescence]]** and bolster immune function (infection defense, surveillance, lower chronic inflammation).

> [!warning] Provenance & framing
> Research/educational only. Thymosin α1 is approved in 35+ countries (not US); the rest are research-use.

## Why it synergises (three layers)
- **Peptides:** [[Thymosin Alpha-1]] (dendritic/T-cell, the lead immune modulator — 11,000+ patients), [[Thymulin]] (zinc-dependent thymic hormone — raise via adequate **zinc**), [[Tuftsin]] (phagocytosis + macrophage M1→M2), [[KPV]] (anti-inflammatory), [[BPC-157]] (gut immunity).
- **Behaviors:** [[Sleep Hygiene]] (immune repair), [[Sauna]]/[[Cold Plunge]] (hormesis), [[Fasting]] (autophagy/immune renewal).

## Timing & intake
- Thymosin α1 ~2.5 mg 2×/wk (travel/illness-season prophylaxis); ensure zinc sufficiency for thymulin; cycle.

## Cautions
- `[warning]` Immune *modulation* (not blanket stimulation); caution with autoimmune conditions; thymulin requires zinc.

## When to consider
- CD4:CD8 ratio <1 (immunosenescence marker, common past 60), recurrent infections, or post-viral immune dysregulation.

## Evidence backing
- [[TRANSCRIPT-top5-longevity-peptides|Longevity Top-5]] (thymosin α1); [[TRANSCRIPT-huberman-bakri-peptides|Bakri]] (thymulin/thymus biology).

## Monitoring
- CD4:CD8, lymphocyte:monocyte ratio, infection frequency.
