# Gut Health Protocol

> [!info] Goal
> Heal/soothe the gut lining — the top-tier use case for oral BPC-157.

> [!warning] Provenance & framing
> Research/educational only. Oral peptides aren't FDA-approved; short, targeted courses.

## Why it synergises (three layers)
- **Peptides:** **oral (arginate-salt) [[BPC-157]]** (gut-lining repair, tight junctions, gut-brain axis — it's a gastric-juice peptide), [[KPV]] (anti-inflammatory, gut/skin), [[GHK-Cu]] (mucosal healing via SIRT1/tight junctions in IBD models).
- **Behaviors:** [[Fasting]] (gut rest, microbiome), [[Sleep Hygiene]] (gut-brain/stress), stress reduction.

## Timing & intake
- Oral BPC-157 ~500 mcg/day + KPV ~500 mcg/day; arginate salt for oral bioavailability; 4–8-week courses.

## Cautions
- `[warning]` Short targeted courses; cancer-caution (BPC/GHK angiogenesis); not indefinite.

## Evidence backing
- [[TRANSCRIPT-bpc157-deep-dive|BPC-157]] (gut data), [[TRANSCRIPT-ghk-cu-deep-dive|GHK-Cu]] (IBD models).

## Monitoring
- GI symptoms / stool panels; cancer screening currency.
