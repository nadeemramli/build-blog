# Endurance / Athletic Performance Protocol

> [!info] Goal
> Improve aerobic/endurance capacity and recovery — peptides **augment** training, they don't replace it.

> [!danger] Provenance & doping note
> Research/educational only. **WADA-banned** for tested athletes: [[MOTS-C]], [[Cardarine]], [[BPC-157]], [[TB-500]], and GH secretagogues. Not for competition.

## Why it synergises (three layers)
- **Peptides:** [[MOTS-C]] (endurance/slow-twitch + mitochondrial — best taken **30 min pre-aerobic**), [[Hexarelin]] (clean morning energy/endurance GH pulse), [[BPC-157]]+[[TB-500]] for recovery between sessions. *(Cardarine is a non-peptide endurance booster but was scrapped for an animal cancer signal — see [[Cardarine]].)*
- **Supplements:** [[NAD]], [[CoQ10]] (mitochondrial support).
- **Behaviors (the actual stimulus):** [[Zone 2 Cardio]] base + [[Endurance Training]] + [[HIIT]]; [[Sauna]] (cardiovascular/heat adaptation); [[Sleep Hygiene]].

## Timing & intake
- MOTS-C 30 min pre-aerobic; hexarelin AM; BPC/TB on recovery days; behaviors are the program.

## Cautions
- `[warning]` Doping bans (above); MOTS-C homocysteine/folate caution; don't use peptides to skip training (you lose the systemic benefits of aerobic exercise).
