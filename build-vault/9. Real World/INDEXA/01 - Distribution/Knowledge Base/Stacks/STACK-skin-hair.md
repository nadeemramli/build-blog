# Skin & Hair Protocol (Aesthetic)

> [!info] Goal
> Regenerate skin (collagen, wrinkles, density) and support hair growth — mostly **topical**, the layer with the best human evidence.

> [!warning] Provenance & framing
> Research/educational only. Injectable copper peptides aren't FDA-approved; topical GHK-Cu isn't banned. Build with a clinician.

## Why it synergises (three layers)
- **Peptides:** [[GHK-Cu]] is the centerpiece (collagen synthesis + remodeling via gene regulation, [[Angiogenesis]]); collagen-mimetic [[Matrixyl]]/[[Tripeptide-29]]; SNARE/"Botox-like" [[SNAP-8]]/[[Acetyl Hexapeptide-3]] for expression lines; [[Decapeptide-12]] for brightening; [[AHK-Cu]] for hair.
- **Behaviors:** **near-infrared/red-light [[Sauna]]** is synergistic with topical GHK-Cu; [[Sleep Hygiene]] (repair); [[Morning Sunlight]] for circadian skin health (with UV caution).

## Timing & intake
- Topical GHK-Cu 0.5–2%, once daily, ideally compounding-pharmacy grade; boost with micro-needling (q2–4 wk) + hyaluronic-acid pre-hydration; pair with red light. SubQ GHK-Cu adds systemic/longevity benefit.

## Cautions
- `[warning]` GHK-Cu injection-site stinging; UV caution; cancer-caution applies to its angiogenesis (lower than BPC, but avoid in active cancer).
