# Mood / Anxiety / Stress Protocol

> [!info] Goal
> Lower anxiety and support mood — [[Selank]] gives benzodiazepine-like anxiolysis **without** tolerance/dependence.

> [!warning] Provenance & framing
> Research/educational only; small/unblinded Russian data. **Serious medication-interaction caution** (see below).

## Why it synergises (three layers)
- **Peptides:** [[Selank]] (GABAergic anxiolysis + immune/anti-inflammatory), [[Semax]] (mood/BDNF), [[BPC-157]] (gut-brain axis → dopamine/serotonin stabilization; note anhedonia reports at higher doses).
- **Supplement:** [[Glycine]] (calming inhibitory neurotransmitter).
- **Behaviors:** [[Zone 2 Cardio]]/[[Resistance Training]] (the strongest evidence-based mood levers), [[Morning Sunlight]], [[Sleep Hygiene]], [[Sauna]].

## Timing & intake
- Selank intranasal, consistently through the cycle (not just acute); behaviors daily.

## Cautions
- `[warning]` [[Selank]] modulates serotonin/GABA/opioid systems → **avoid combining with SSRIs/SNRIs/benzos/alcohol** without close medical supervision (serotonin-syndrome/sedation risk). BPC-157 anhedonia reports.

## Evidence backing
- [[TRANSCRIPT-selank-deep-dive|Selank]] (anxiety RCTs vs benzodiazepines).

## Monitoring
- Mood/anxiety scales; medication review.
