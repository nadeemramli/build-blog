# Sexual Health / Libido / Vitality Protocol

> [!info] Goal
> Improve libido/sexual function (central + hormonal) and overall vitality.

> [!warning] Provenance & framing
> Research/educational only. PT-141 (Vyleesi) is FDA-approved for female hypoactive desire; others are research-use/off-label.

## Why it synergises (three layers)
- **Peptides:** [[PT-141]] (central, **neurogenic** libido via [[Melanocortin Agonism]]), [[Melanotan II]] (MSH → libido + tanning), [[Kisspeptin-10]] (upstream HPG → testosterone/vitality), [[HCG]] (LH analog → endogenous testosterone). Acts at brain + hormonal levels, not just blood flow.
- **Behaviors:** [[Sleep Hygiene]], [[Resistance Training]], [[Morning Sunlight]] (the hormonal foundation).

## Timing & intake
- PT-141 as-needed (narrow window — nausea if over-dosed); kisspeptin/HCG per HPG protocols.

## Cautions
- `[warning]` PT-141 nausea / blood-pressure rise; [[Melanotan II]] melanoma caution + unnatural tan; HCG only helps secondary (not primary) hypogonadism.

## Evidence backing
- [[TRANSCRIPT-huberman-peptides-solo|Huberman]] (melanocortin/kisspeptin), [[TRANSCRIPT-trt-testosterone-optimization|TRT guide]] (HPG/HCG).

## Monitoring
- Blood pressure (PT-141); hormone labs if using kisspeptin/HCG.
