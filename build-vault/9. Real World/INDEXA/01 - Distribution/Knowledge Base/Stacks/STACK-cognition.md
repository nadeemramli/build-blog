# Cognition / Nootropic Protocol

> [!info] Goal
> Sharpen focus/memory and protect the brain.

> [!warning] Provenance & framing
> Research/educational only; peptide claims largely Russian/anecdote. Build with a clinician — note serotonergic interactions below.

## Why it synergises (three layers)
- **Peptides:** [[Semax]] (BDNF/focus/neuroprotection — the lead nootropic), [[Selank]] (calm, distraction-reducing focus), [[Pinealon]] taken **in the morning** (daytime alertness/HIIT performance).
- **Supplements:** [[Methylene Blue]] (mitochondrial + mild MAOI cognitive lift), [[NAD]] (neuronal energy).
- **Behaviors:** exercise ([[Resistance Training]]/[[Zone 2 Cardio]] → BDNF), [[Sleep Hygiene]], [[Morning Sunlight]], [[Fasting]] (BDNF/ketones).

## Timing & intake
- Semax/Selank intranasal (brain-targeted); pinealon AM; methylene blue ~10 mg AM; exercise daytime.

## Cautions
- `[warning]` **Methylene blue is an MAOI** and [[Selank]] modulates serotonin → avoid stacking with SSRIs/SNRIs (serotonin-syndrome risk) without medical supervision.
