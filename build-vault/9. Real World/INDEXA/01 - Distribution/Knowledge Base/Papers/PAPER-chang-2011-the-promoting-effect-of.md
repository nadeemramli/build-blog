# The promoting effect of BPC 157 on tendon healing

## Key finding


BPC-157 promoted tendon-fibroblast outgrowth, survival under stress and migration via the FAK-paxillin pathway, and increased growth-hormone-receptor expression in tendon fibroblasts — a mechanism for accelerated tendon healing.

## Method / model


Rat Achilles tendon explant + fibroblast culture (in vitro/ex vivo).

## Relevance


Key tendon-healing mechanism for [[BPC-157]].
