# Melanocortin-derived tripeptide KPV has anti-inflammatory potential in murine models of inflammatory bowel disease

## Key finding


The melanocortin-derived tripeptide KPV showed significant anti-inflammatory activity in two murine colitis models (DSS and CD45RBhi transfer), with effects at least partly independent of MC1R signalling.

## Method / model


Two murine IBD models.

## Relevance


Mechanistic basis for [[KPV]] anti-inflammatory / gut claims.
