# Human telomere biology

## Key finding


Telomere attrition acts as a contributory, interactive factor in aging and disease risk — short telomeres block cell division and impair tissue renewal, while telomere maintenance is protective.

## Method / model


Review by the Nobel-laureate telomere group (Blackburn, Epel, Lin).

## Relevance


Evidence base for telomere-targeting longevity claims ([[Epithalon]], [[Hallmarks of Aging]]).
