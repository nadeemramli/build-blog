# Prolonged stimulation of growth hormone (GH) and insulin-like growth factor I by CJC-1295

## Key finding


In healthy adults, a single CJC-1295 injection raised plasma GH 2-10x for 6+ days and IGF-1 1.5-3x for 9-11 days (estimated half-life 5.8-8.1 days); safe and well tolerated at 30-60 mcg/kg.

## Method / model


Randomised, double-blind, placebo-controlled trial in healthy adults.

## Relevance


Human PK/PD evidence for long-acting GHRH analogs ([[CJC-1295 + Ipamorelin]]).
