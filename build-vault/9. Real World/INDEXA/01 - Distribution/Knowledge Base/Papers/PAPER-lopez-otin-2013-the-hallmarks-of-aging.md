# The hallmarks of aging

## Key finding


Defines the nine hallmarks of aging (genomic instability, telomere attrition, epigenetic alterations, loss of proteostasis, deregulated nutrient sensing, mitochondrial dysfunction, cellular senescence, stem-cell exhaustion, altered intercellular communication) — the organising framework for longevity science.

Landmark consensus review.
