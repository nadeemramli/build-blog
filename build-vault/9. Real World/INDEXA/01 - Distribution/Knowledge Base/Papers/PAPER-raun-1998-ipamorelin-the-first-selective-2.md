# Ipamorelin, the first selective growth hormone secretagogue

## Key finding


Characterises ipamorelin (Aib-His-D-2-Nal-D-Phe-Lys-NH2) as the first GH secretagogue to release GH with potency comparable to GHRP-6 but WITHOUT elevating ACTH or cortisol — establishing its selectivity advantage.

## Method / model


In-vitro pituitary cell and in-vivo rat/swine studies.

## Relevance


Selectivity basis for [[Ipamorelin]] preference in GH-axis stacks.
