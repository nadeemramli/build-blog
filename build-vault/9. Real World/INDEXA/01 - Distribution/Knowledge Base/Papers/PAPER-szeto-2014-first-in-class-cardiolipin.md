# First-in-class cardiolipin-protective compound as a therapeutic agent

## Key finding


SS-31 (elamipretide) selectively concentrates in the inner mitochondrial membrane and binds cardiolipin, protecting cristae architecture and restoring oxidative phosphorylation under stress — defining the first-in-class cardiolipin-targeted mitoprotective mechanism.

## Method / model


Review of the SS-peptide pharmacology and cardiolipin-binding data.

## Relevance


Foundational mechanism reference for [[SS-31]] and [[Mitochondrial Bioenergetics]].
