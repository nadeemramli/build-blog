# Mechanical signals, IGF-I gene splicing, and muscle adaptation

## Key finding


Mechanical loading drives alternative splicing of the IGF-1 gene to mechano-growth factor (MGF/IGF-1Ec), which 'kick-starts' local muscle hypertrophy and repair; this splicing capacity is lost with age and disease.

## Method / model


Review of IGF-1 splicing and muscle adaptation.

## Relevance


Mechanistic basis for IGF-1/MGF muscle-repair claims ([[IGF-1 LR3]], [[IGF-1 Signaling]]).
