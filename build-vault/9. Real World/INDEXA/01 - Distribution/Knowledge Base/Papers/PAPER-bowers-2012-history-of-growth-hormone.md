# History of growth hormone-releasing peptides

## Key finding


First-hand historical account of the discovery of growth-hormone-releasing peptides (GHRPs) and the path to ghrelin — establishing the GHS-receptor/ghrelin axis that GHRPs act on.

## Method / model


Historical review by the GHRP discoverer.

Origin reference for the GHRP class ([[GHRP-6]], [[GHRP-2]], [[Hexarelin]], [[Ipamorelin]]).
