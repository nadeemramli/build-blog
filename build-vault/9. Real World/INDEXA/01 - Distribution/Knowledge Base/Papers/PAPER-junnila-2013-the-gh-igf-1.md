# The GH/IGF-1 axis in ageing and longevity

## Key finding


GH/IGF-1 secretion declines with age; paradoxically, mutations that LOWER GH/IGF-1 tone are associated with extended longevity in mice — framing the trade-off between GH-driven anabolism and lifespan.

## Method / model


Review of GH/IGF-1 axis genetics and aging.

## Relevance


Key caveat reference balancing GH-secretagogue stacks against [[STACK-longevity|longevity]] goals.
