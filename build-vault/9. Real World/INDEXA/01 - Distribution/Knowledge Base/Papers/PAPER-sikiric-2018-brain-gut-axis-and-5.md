# Brain-gut axis and pentadecapeptide BPC 157: Theoretical and practical implications

## Key finding


Comprehensive review establishing BPC-157 as a stable gastric pentadecapeptide that acts along the gut-brain axis — cytoprotective/angiogenic in the GI tract and neuroprotective centrally (stroke, TBI, nerve healing), with no toxicity reported in animal models.

## Method / model


Narrative review of the animal BPC-157 literature.

## Relevance


Anchor mechanism reference for [[BPC-157]] across gut, vascular and neuro effects.
