# The Safety and Efficacy of Growth Hormone Secretagogues

## Key finding


Reviews GH secretagogues (GHRPs and oral MK-677); notes they can increase GH/IGF-1, lean mass, appetite and sleep quality, but flags the scarcity of long-term rigorously controlled safety data.

## Method / model


Literature review.

## Relevance


Balanced safety/efficacy reference across GH-secretagogue stacks ([[MK-677]], [[Ipamorelin]]).
