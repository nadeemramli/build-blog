# Metabolic Effects of Tesamorelin in HIV-Associated Lipodystrophy

## Key finding


In 412 HIV patients with abdominal fat accumulation, 2 mg/day tesamorelin for 26 weeks significantly reduced visceral adipose tissue and improved triglycerides vs placebo, without worsening glucose.

## Method / model


Phase 3 randomised, double-blind, placebo-controlled trial.

## Relevance


Pivotal human RCT for tesamorelin (GHRH analog) visceral-fat reduction.
