# Epithalon Peptide Induces Telomerase Activity and Telomere Elongation in Human Somatic Cells

## Key finding


Epithalon (Ala-Glu-Asp-Gly) induced telomerase activity and ~2.4x telomere elongation in human somatic (fetal fibroblast) cells, extending divisions by ~42.5% — evidence of overcoming the Hayflick limit in vitro.

## Method / model


Human fetal fibroblast cell culture.

## Relevance


Core in-vitro support for [[Epithalon]] telomere claims (note: human cell line, not in-vivo).
