# Thymosin beta4 activates integrin-linked kinase and promotes cardiac cell migration, survival and cardiac repair

## Key finding


Thymosin beta-4 forms a complex with PINCH and integrin-linked kinase (ILK), activating Akt; after coronary ligation in mice it enhanced cardiomyocyte survival and improved cardiac function — defining its cardioprotective repair mechanism.

## Method / model


Embryonic/postnatal cardiomyocyte culture + mouse MI model.

## Relevance


Foundational repair mechanism for [[TB-500]]/Thymosin beta-4.
