# CagriSema Phase 3 REDEFINE-1/-2: first-in-class amylin/GLP-1 combo, up to 22.7% weight loss

> [!info] Provenance: two Phase 3 RCTs — presented at a recognised conference AND peer-reviewed
> **REDEFINE-1** and **REDEFINE-2** presented at the **ADA 2025 Scientific Sessions** (late-breaking) and **simultaneously published in NEJM** (REDEFINE-1 NEJMoa2502081 / PMID 40544433; REDEFINE-2 NEJMoa2502082 / PMID 40544432). CagriSema = a once-weekly fixed combination of **cagrilintide** (long-acting **amylin** analog) + **semaglutide** (GLP-1 RA). Recorded now to fill an atomic-graph gap and because the FDA decision lands in 2026.

## Key findings
- **REDEFINE-1** — 68-week Phase 3, **3,400 adults** with overweight/obesity **without diabetes** (≥1 weight-related condition); randomized to **CagriSema (cagrilintide 2.4 mg + semaglutide 2.4 mg)** vs semaglutide 2.4 mg vs cagrilintide 2.4 mg vs placebo, once-weekly. Mean weight loss at wk 68 (treatment-policy): **CagriSema 20.4%** vs **semaglutide 14.9%**, **cagrilintide 11.5%**, **placebo 3%**. Full-adherence (trial-product) estimand: **22.7%**, with **40.4% achieving ≥25%** weight loss.
- **REDEFINE-2** — 68-week, ~**1,200 adults with type 2 diabetes**: **13.7%** (treatment-policy) / **15.7%** (trial-product) vs ~3% placebo. 88% of prediabetics returned to normoglycemia (REDEFINE-1).
- Both trials **met their primary endpoint**. First-in-class once-weekly **GLP-1 + amylin** combination; safety consistent with the GLP-1 class (mostly mild-to-moderate GI).
- **Regulatory:** Novo Nordisk **filed a US NDA in Dec 2025** for weight management on the REDEFINE-1/-2 data; **FDA decision anticipated late 2026**. A higher-dose CagriSema (2.4/7.2 mg) Phase 3 is planned for H2-2026; **REDEFINE 11** (full weight-loss potential) expected to report H1-2027.

## Caveats
- **Not FDA-approved** (under review; decision expected late 2026). **Estimand split** — cite the conservative **treatment-policy 20.4%** as the headline, not the 22.7% trial-product figure. Sponsor-run; REDEFINE-1's topline drew market scrutiny for landing below an internal ≥25% expectation. Research/educational use only.
