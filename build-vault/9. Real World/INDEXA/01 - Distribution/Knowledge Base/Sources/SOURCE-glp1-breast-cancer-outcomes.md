# GLP-1 RAs linked to better breast-cancer outcomes in obesity or T2D (JAMA Network Open 2026)

> [!info] Provenance: peer-reviewed journal via a reputable oncology network
> Retrospective cohort led by **Virginia Commonwealth University Massey Comprehensive Cancer Center**, **published in JAMA Network Open (May 11, 2026)**; reported by Oncology Central. **Observational** — association only.

## Key findings
- EHR analysis of **>840,000 breast-cancer patients** (2006–2023).
- **In obesity:** GLP-1 RA use → lower all-cause mortality (**HR 0.35**) and better recurrence-free survival (**HR 0.44**) over 10 years.
- **In type-2 diabetes:** vs insulin/metformin → all-cause mortality **HR 0.09** and recurrence-free survival **HR 0.33**.

## Why it matters for this base
- Reinforces the [[GLP-1 Agonism|GLP-1]] **cancer-protective** signal (alongside [[SOURCE-asco2026-glp1-cancer-progression]]) and the metabolism→cancer-outcome link — relevant to the [[STACK-longevity|Longevity protocol]].

## Caveats
- Observational; the authors stress **randomized trials are needed** before claiming GLP-1 RAs *directly* improve cancer outcomes.
