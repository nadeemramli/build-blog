# Senolysis

## What it is
- `[educational]` Selective clearing of **senescent cells** — cells that have stopped dividing but resist death and secrete inflammatory factors (the SASP) that damage surrounding tissue and drive aging (a hallmark of aging). Senolytics push these cells into apoptosis. — [[TRANSCRIPT-top5-longevity-peptides|Longevity Top-5]]
