## Evidence

- `[literature]` The glucagon arm is the differentiator behind retatrutide's class-leading **Phase 3 weight loss (up to 28.3%, TRIUMPH-1 2026)** vs GLP-1/GIP-only agents — adding energy expenditure/hepatic-fat effects atop appetite suppression. — [[SOURCE-retatrutide-triumph1-phase3]]

- `[literature]` The glucagon-arm's **hepatic/visceral-fat** signature shows clearly in the glucagon/GLP-1 dual agonist **survodutide** (SYNCHRONIZE-1, ADA 2026): 16.6% weight loss but visceral fat ↓34% and **liver fat ↓63%** — glucagon receptor activation targeting the liver, not just appetite. — [[SOURCE-survodutide-synchronize1-phase3-2026]]
