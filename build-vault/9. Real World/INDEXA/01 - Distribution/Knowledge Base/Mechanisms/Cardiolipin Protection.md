## What it is


- `[educational]` **Cardiolipin** is the inner-mitochondrial-membrane phospholipid that holds the **electron transport chain** stable. Reactive oxygen species damage it → ETC instability → more ROS → a **vicious cycle** of mitochondrial dysfunction (a hallmark of aging). [[SS-31]] binds/stabilizes cardiolipin to break this cycle (structural, not signaling). — [[TRANSCRIPT-ss31-deep-dive|SS-31 deep dive]]
