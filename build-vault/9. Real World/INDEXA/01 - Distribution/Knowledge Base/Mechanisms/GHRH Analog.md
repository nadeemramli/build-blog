## What it is


- `[educational]` GHRH = 44-aa hypothalamic peptide → pituitary GH. Analogs: tesamorelin (44 aa + trans-hexenoic acid), sermorelin (first 29 aa), CJC-1295 (29 aa + 4 changes ± DAC). GHRH desensitizes far less than GHRPs *and* upregulates GHSR synthesis (enabling GHRH+GHRP synergy) → it's the **base** of any stack. — [[TRANSCRIPT-gh-peptide-selection-deep-dive|GH selection guide]]

## Evidence



- `[anecdote-bb]` GHRH release is pulsatile, rising with **low blood glucose** and during sleep; inhibited by circulating IGF-1 and GABAergic neurons. GHRH-analog secretagogues exploit this rhythm. — [[TRANSCRIPT-vigorous-steve-gh-secretagogues|Vigorous Steve]]

- `[educational]` Night dosing yields a bigger GH spike and **less receptor desensitization** than daytime (lower nighttime somatostatin); GH peaks ~30–45 min after SubQ. Oral GHRH ≈ 0% bioavailable; the pituitary sits *outside* the blood-brain barrier, so **systemic (SubQ) delivery** is the goal. — [[TRANSCRIPT-gh-peptide-administration-deep-dive|GH admin guide]]
