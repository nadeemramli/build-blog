## Evidence


- `[educational]` TB4's core mechanism: its aa 17–23 segment (= the TB500 fragment) binds **G-actin**, stabilising actin building blocks for **cell migration** and **angiogenesis** (endothelial cells lining new vessels) — the basis of its tissue-repair effect. — [[TRANSCRIPT-tb4-tb500-deep-dive|TB4/TB500 deep dive]]

- `[literature]` The actin-binding **17–23 fragment** itself is now in a registered Phase 1/2 human trial (TB-500 vs placebo, stable ASCVD, 2026, no results yet). — [[SOURCE-tb500-ascvd-rct-nct07487363]]
