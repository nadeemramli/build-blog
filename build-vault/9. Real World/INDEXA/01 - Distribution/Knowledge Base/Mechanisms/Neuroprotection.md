## Evidence
- `[literature]` **GLP-1 neuroprotection — a Phase 3 null in Alzheimer's (EVOKE / EVOKE+, Lancet 2026):** the most-hyped GLP-1 "brain" claim was tested head-on — oral [[Semaglutide]] 14 mg in 3,808 amyloid-positive early-AD patients showed **no cognitive or functional benefit** vs placebo (CDR-SB primary missed at 104 wk), despite a ~30% CRP reduction. A cautionary datapoint: anti-inflammatory / mechanistic neuro signals need not translate into clinical neuroprotection. — [[SOURCE-semaglutide-evoke-alzheimers-phase3-2026]]
