# GnRH

## Definition
- `[educational]` Gonadotropin-releasing hormone — the hypothalamic signal that drives pituitary **LH/FSH** release.

## Why it matters
- `[educational]` The relay between [[Kisspeptin-10]] (upstream) and [[Gonadotropin Activity]] (LH/FSH → testosterone/sperm); SERMs ([[Clomiphene]]/[[Enclomiphene]]) raise it by blocking hypothalamic estrogen feedback.
