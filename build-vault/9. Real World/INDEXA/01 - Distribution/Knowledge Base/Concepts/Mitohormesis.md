# Mitohormesis

## Definition
- `[educational]` The 'come-back-stronger' adaptation: a mild mitochondrial stressor triggers compensatory upgrades to mitochondrial function.

## Why it matters
- `[educational]` The framing for [[MOTS-C]] as 'exercise in a vial' — and the rationale for **intermittent** dosing/cycling (stress → recover → adapt) rather than continuous use.
