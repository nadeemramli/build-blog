## Why it matters


- `[anecdote-bb]` Secretagogues (GHRH-analogs + GHRPs) coax the pituitary to release *its own* GH, so output is capped by pituitary capacity and blunted by somatostatin/IGF-1 feedback. Bodybuilding consensus across sources: useful but **modest vs exogenous GH**, which raises serum GH/IGF-1 far more per dollar — especially past ~age 40. — [[TRANSCRIPT-vigorous-steve-gh-secretagogues|Vigorous Steve]]
