## Why it matters


- `[educational]` GH-peptide template: **start low, go slow**; individual dose > total daily dose (pulsatile + short half-lives); night-time, fasted ~4 h; max the nightly dose before adding a daytime dose; **5-on/2-off** and **3–4 mo on / 1 mo off** cycles to resensitize. — [[TRANSCRIPT-gh-peptide-administration-deep-dive|GH admin guide]]
- For translating animal-study doses into human-equivalent doses, see the method note [[Animal-to-Human Dose Conversion]].
