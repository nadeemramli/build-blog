# AMPK

## Definition
- `[educational]` AMP-activated protein kinase — the cell's master **energy sensor/regulator**, activated when energy is low.

## Why it matters
- `[educational]` [[MOTS-C]] (and exercise, fasting, metformin) activate AMPK → [[SIRT1]]/PGC-1α → mito biogenesis, fat/glucose handling, ↓inflammation; the catabolic/repair counterweight to mTOR growth signaling.
