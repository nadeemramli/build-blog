# SIRT1

## Definition
- `[educational]` A **sirtuin** — an NAD-dependent enzyme central to cellular repair, metabolism, and longevity signaling.

## Why it matters
- `[educational]` Downstream of [[AMPK]]; engaged by [[MOTS-C]], [[GHK-Cu]], and the low-GH-trough state. Part of why returning GH to a low baseline (vs CJC-DAC) matters for longevity.
