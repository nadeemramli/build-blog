# DPP-4

## Definition
- `[educational]` Dipeptidyl peptidase-4 — the enzyme that rapidly degrades **GLP-1** (native half-life ~2 min) and other peptides.

## Why it matters
- `[educational]` GLP-1 drugs are engineered for DPP-4 resistance (the basis of [[Semaglutide]]'s long half-life); DPP-4 *inhibitors* are sometimes misused to extend injectable GH-peptide half-life (thin evidence — see Vigorous Steve transcript).
