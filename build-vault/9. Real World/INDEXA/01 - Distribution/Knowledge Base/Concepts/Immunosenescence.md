# Immunosenescence

## Definition
- `[educational]` The age-related decline of the immune system — thymic involution + dysfunctional T/B cells + chronic inflammation.

## Why it matters
- `[educational]` A driver of cancer/infection/autoimmunity risk with age; [[Thymosin Alpha-1]] aims to counter it. A practical marker: CD4:CD8 ratio <1 (common past 60).
