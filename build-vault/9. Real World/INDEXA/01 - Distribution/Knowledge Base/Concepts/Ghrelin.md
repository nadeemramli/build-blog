# Ghrelin

## Definition
- `[educational]` The stomach 'hunger hormone'; a natural agonist of the **growth-hormone-secretagogue receptor (GHSR-1A)**, released with fasting.

## Why it matters
- `[educational]` Ghrelin amplifies GHRH-driven GH release and inhibits [[Somatostatin]]; the GHRPs ([[Ipamorelin]], [[GHRP-6]], [[GHRP-2]], [[Hexarelin]]) and [[MK-677]] mimic it — hence their shared hunger side effect.
