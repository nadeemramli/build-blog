# Somatostatin

## Definition
- `[educational]` The hormone (a.k.a. GHIH) that **inhibits growth-hormone release**; rises with eating and with high IGF-1.

## Why it matters
- `[educational]` It's the brake on the GH axis — lowest at night (enabling the big pulse) and raised by food/feedback. Why GH peptides are dosed at night, fasted, and why [[GHRH Analog]]/[[Ghrelin-GHRP Agonism]] agents work better when somatostatin is low.
