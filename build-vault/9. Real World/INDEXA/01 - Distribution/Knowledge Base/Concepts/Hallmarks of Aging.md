# Hallmarks of Aging

## Definition
- `[educational]` The ~12 root-cause processes of aging (genomic instability, telomere attrition, epigenetic alterations, mitochondrial dysfunction, cellular senescence, inflammation, etc.).

## Why it matters
- `[educational]` The lens for ranking longevity peptides: [[FOXO4-DRI]] → senescence, [[Epithalon]] → telomeres/epigenetics, [[MOTS-C]]/[[SS-31]] → mitochondria, GLP-1s → multiple. The more hallmarks a peptide targets, the stronger its longevity rationale.
