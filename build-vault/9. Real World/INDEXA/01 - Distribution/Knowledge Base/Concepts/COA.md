## Definition

## Why it matters


- `[educational]` A trustworthy peptide **certificate of analysis** is **batch-specific** and includes: **HPLC + mass-spec** identity/purity (target >99%), **USP<85> endotoxin**, and **USP<71> sterility** (heavy-metals a bonus). Injectables need all of these; oral forms need purity/identity (the gut tolerates small endotoxin/microbe loads). — [[TRANSCRIPT-bpc157-deep-dive|BPC-157 deep dive]]
