# NF-κB

## Definition
- `[educational]` A master transcription-factor regulator of **inflammatory** gene expression.

## Why it matters
- `[educational]` Suppressed by [[GHK-Cu]] and [[MOTS-C]] → lower IL-6/TNF-α/inflammation; chronic NF-κB activity drives many age-related diseases.
