# Nrf2

## Definition
- `[educational]` The master regulator of the cellular **antioxidant** response.

## Why it matters
- `[educational]` Up-regulated by [[GHK-Cu]] (→ SOD and other endogenous antioxidants); pairs with NF-κB suppression as the anti-inflammatory/antioxidant axis behind many peptide longevity claims.
