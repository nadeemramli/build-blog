# TB-500 (Thymosin Beta-4): Healing & Regeneration Research

*Research documentation on the actin-sequestering peptide for tissue repair*

> **Source:** https://indexalabs.com/blog/tb-500-thymosin-beta-4-healing-research
> **Abstract:** TB-500, the active region of Thymosin Beta-4, is a key peptide in tissue repair research. This guide covers its unique mechanisms, research applications, and synergistic use with other healing peptides.

## 1. Overview

TB-500 is a synthetic version of the active region of Thymosin Beta-4, a naturally occurring 43-amino acid peptide present in high concentrations in wound fluid and blood platelets. TB-500 promotes cell migration, blood vessel formation, and tissue regeneration.

## 2. Mechanisms of Action

**2.1 Actin Regulation**
TB-500 sequesters G-actin, promoting cell motility and migration essential for tissue repair.

**2.2 Angiogenesis**
Promotes new blood vessel formation in damaged tissues.

**2.3 Anti-Inflammatory Effects**
Downregulates inflammatory cytokines while promoting healing pathways.

**2.4 Stem Cell Recruitment**
May enhance stem cell migration to injury sites.

## 3. Research Applications

**3.1 Cardiac Research**
Extensive research on cardiac repair following ischemic injury.

**3.2 Dermal Healing**
Studies on accelerated wound healing and reduced scarring.

**3.3 Musculoskeletal Repair**
Research on muscle, tendon, and ligament healing.

**3.4 Corneal Healing**
Studies showing accelerated corneal repair.

## 4. Synergistic Combinations

**4.1 TB-500 + BPC-157**
The most popular combination, addressing different healing pathways for comprehensive tissue repair.

**4.2 Triple Combination**
Adding GHK-Cu provides additional collagen support and anti-inflammatory benefits.

## 5. Research Protocols

**5.1 Available Sizes**
TB-500 is available in 5mg vials.

**5.2 Loading vs. Maintenance**
Research often uses higher initial doses followed by maintenance protocols.

**5.3 Storage**
Lyophilized TB-500 is stable at room temperature short-term but should be refrigerated for longer storage.
