# AHK-Cu Raw Powder: Copper Tripeptide for Hair & Skin Research

*Understanding AHK-Cu applications in hair follicle and dermal research*

> **Source:** https://indexalabs.com/blog/ahk-cu-raw-powder-hair-skin-research
> **Abstract:** AHK-Cu (Alanine-Histidine-Lysine Copper) is a biomimetic copper peptide with research focus on hair follicle stimulation and skin regeneration, offering complementary benefits to GHK-Cu.

## 1. Overview & Background

**1.1 What is AHK-Cu?**
AHK-Cu is a synthetic copper tripeptide designed for enhanced hair follicle penetration and dermal activity.

**1.2 Structural Differences from GHK-Cu**
The alanine substitution provides different receptor binding characteristics while maintaining copper delivery capabilities.

**1.3 Research Focus**
Primarily studied for hair growth stimulation, though skin regeneration applications are also being explored.

## 2. Mechanisms of Action

**2.1 Hair Follicle Stimulation**
Research indicates AHK-Cu may extend the anagen (growth) phase of hair follicles and increase follicle size.

**2.2 Dermal Papilla Activation**
Studies suggest stimulation of dermal papilla cells, which regulate hair follicle cycling.

**2.3 ECM Support**
Like GHK-Cu, supports extracellular matrix proteins essential for both skin and hair follicle structure.

**2.4 Copper-Dependent Enzymes**
Activates copper-dependent enzymes involved in melanin synthesis and tissue repair.

## 3. Research Applications

**3.1 Hair Loss Research**
Investigation into androgenetic alopecia and other hair thinning conditions.

**3.2 Scalp Health**
Potential applications in scalp inflammation and folliculitis research.

**3.3 Combination Studies**
Often studied alongside other hair growth peptides and minoxidil.

**3.4 Skin Regeneration**
Secondary research focus on wound healing and anti-aging applications.

## 4. Formulation Considerations

**4.1 Concentration**
Typical research concentrations range from 50-500 ppm in formulations.

**4.2 Delivery Systems**
Liposomal and nanoparticle delivery systems being studied for enhanced follicular penetration.

**4.3 Stability**
Store powder desiccated at -20°C. Protect from light and moisture.

**4.4 Synergistic Ingredients**
Research into combinations with biotin, caffeine, and saw palmetto extracts.
