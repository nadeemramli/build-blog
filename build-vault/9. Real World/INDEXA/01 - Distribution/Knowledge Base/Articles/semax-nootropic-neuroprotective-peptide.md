# Semax: Nootropic & Neuroprotective Peptide

*Research on the ACTH-derived peptide for cognitive enhancement and brain health*

> **Source:** https://indexalabs.com/blog/semax-nootropic-neuroprotective-peptide
> **Abstract:** Semax is a synthetic ACTH analog developed in Russia with potent nootropic and neuroprotective properties. This guide explores its mechanisms, research evidence, and applications in cognitive enhancement.

## 1. Overview

Semax (Met-Glu-His-Phe-Pro-Gly-Pro) is a heptapeptide based on the ACTH(4-10) fragment with a C-terminal tripeptide extension (Pro-Gly-Pro) that enhances stability and neurotropic activity. It is approved in Russia for stroke recovery and cognitive enhancement.

## 2. Mechanisms of Action

**2.1 BDNF & NGF Expression**
Semax significantly increases brain-derived neurotrophic factor and nerve growth factor expression.

**2.2 Melanocortin System**
Activates melanocortin receptors involved in learning, memory, and neuroprotection.

**2.3 Dopamine Modulation**
Enhances dopaminergic transmission, improving focus and motivation.

**2.4 Neuroprotection**
Provides protection against oxidative stress, excitotoxicity, and ischemic damage.

## 3. Research Evidence

**3.1 Stroke Recovery**
Clinical studies in Russia demonstrated improved outcomes in ischemic stroke patients.

**3.2 Cognitive Enhancement**
Research shows improvements in memory, attention, and learning in various models.

**3.3 ADHD Research**
Studies on attention and impulse control in ADHD models.

**3.4 Neurodegenerative Models**
Research in Alzheimer's and Parkinson's disease models.

## 4. Research Protocols

**4.1 Available Sizes**
Semax is available in 10mg vials.

**4.2 Intranasal Administration**
Most research uses intranasal delivery for direct CNS effects.

**4.3 No Hormonal Effects**
Unlike full-length ACTH, Semax does not stimulate adrenal cortisol production.
