# SS-31 (Elamipretide)

## Overview


- `[educational]` **Elamipretide** — a mitochondrial-*targeted* synthetic tetrapeptide that penetrates to the inner mitochondrial membrane and **binds/stabilizes cardiolipin** → stable electron transport chain → ↑ATP, ↓ROS (breaks the mito-dysfunction vicious cycle). **Now FDA-approved for Barth syndrome** (168-wk trial: +30–40% muscle strength, ~50% heart-function markers) — its only approved use. — [[TRANSCRIPT-ss31-deep-dive|SS-31 deep dive]]

## Mechanisms

## Evidence


- `[educational]` Off-label (mostly preclinical): dementia/cognitive decline & **TBI** (mouse), **chronic kidney disease** (mouse models + a human phase-2 in renal-artery stenting), diabetes/insulin resistance (mouse), chronic fatigue/long COVID (anecdotal + ↑ATP), anti-aging healthspan (mouse). Cardiolipin declines with age; benefit appears from ~human-50 onward (no effect in young mice). — [[TRANSCRIPT-ss31-deep-dive|SS-31 deep dive]]

## Anecdotes




- `[anecdote-bb]` 5 mg ×5 days/wk × ~3 wks gave no obvious subjective benefit atop the mito stack; best-claimed use is **pre-surgery** (raising mitochondrial function/density before extensive procedures). — [[TRANSCRIPT-anabolic-roundtable-peptides|Anabolic Roundtable]] ~01:49–01:53

- `[educational]` Longevity **#6 (honorable mention)** — provides **structural** (cardiolipin) support to mitochondria vs MOTS-C's training effect; has more human safety data. A tossup with MOTS-C. — [[TRANSCRIPT-top5-longevity-peptides|Longevity Top-5]]

- `[educational]` For longevity the creator picks [[MOTS-C]] over SS-31 because MOTS-C benefits mice of **all ages** (trains mitochondria) whereas SS-31 mainly provides **structural (cardiolipin) support** that's needed with aging — though SS-31 has more human safety data. — [[TRANSCRIPT-mots-c-deep-dive|MOTS-C deep dive]]

## Physician protocols


- `[protocol]` SubQ (no oral — 4 aa too large for di/tripeptide gut transporters). Human trials 40 mg/day (extreme-dysfunction populations); for age-related use **start 5–10 mg/day**, titrate by 5 mg to max 40 (cost ~$1/mg). **Daily** dosing (cleared <24 h; structural role). Anti-aging cycle **1 month on / 1 off**; use in 50s+ or when mito labs show dysfunction. — [[TRANSCRIPT-ss31-deep-dive|SS-31 deep dive]]

## Practical notes


- `[educational]` **Notably clean safety** — extensive human data (Barth 168 wk, myopathy/AMD/heart-failure trials) with only injection-site reactions; no receptors/signaling → **no cancer, immune, or mitochondrial-overstress concern** (unlike [[MOTS-C]]); little tolerance/tachyphylaxis. — [[TRANSCRIPT-ss31-deep-dive|SS-31 deep dive]]
