## Anecdotes



- `[anecdote]` Regarded as the best immune-modulator — dial **down** autoimmune overactivity (lupus, RA, celiac, type-1 diabetes) or dial **up** a lagging immune system (long COVID). — [[TRANSCRIPT-huberman-koniver-peptides|Koniver]] 01:41

- `[anecdote]` Thymic-family hormone (≥21 aa) — 'jet fuel for T-cells' (development + proper pathogen targeting). FDA-approved historically as **Zadaxin** (thymus-deficient children); abroad an adjuvant for hepatitis B/C and some cancers (Goldstein-lab 'fraction 5'). — [[TRANSCRIPT-huberman-bakri-peptides|Bakri]] 01:35–01:36

- `[educational]` Ranked **#3 longevity** — counters **immunosenescence** (dendritic TLR activation + IDO immune-tolerance). 11,000+ patients, approved in 35+ countries; severe-COVID mortality 30%→11%; NSCLC −45% death risk. Gap: evidence is in the immunocompromised, not healthy-aging prevention. Consider when **CD4:CD8 <1** (common past 60). — [[TRANSCRIPT-top5-longevity-peptides|Longevity Top-5]]
