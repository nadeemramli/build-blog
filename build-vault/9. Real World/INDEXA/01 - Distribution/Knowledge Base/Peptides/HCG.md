## Anecdotes


- `[anecdote]` Acts as synthetic **LH** (LH/hCG receptor); used for male fertility restoration and female IVF. In animal data, **thymulin sensitises the gonad** so hCG yields *more* testosterone. Recently removed from compounders. — [[TRANSCRIPT-huberman-bakri-peptides|Bakri]] 01:47, 02:35

## Physician protocols


- `[protocol]` Used to restore the HPG axis / sperm production after testosterone-induced suppression; combined with clomiphene and other agents. — [[TRANSCRIPT-huberman-koniver-peptides|Koniver]] 00:33–00:34

- `[protocol]` As an **LH analog** for endogenous-testosterone support / fertility preservation (vs TRT, which suppresses the axis): **500 IU 2×/wk → up to 1,000 IU 2×/wk**. Best for **secondary** hypogonadism (low/normal LH/FSH) and age <45; won't fix **primary** (failing testes). Doesn't raise sperm (LH-only, no FSH — Clomid does). Also used to **reboot** production after TRT/steroids. — [[TRANSCRIPT-trt-testosterone-optimization|TRT/testosterone guide]]
