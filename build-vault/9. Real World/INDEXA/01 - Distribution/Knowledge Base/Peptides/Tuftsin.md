# Tuftsin

## Overview
- `[educational]` A naturally occurring **4-aa fragment of immunoglobulin G (IgG)** — the immune-active parent of [[Selank]] (which is stabilized tuftsin + 3 aa). — [[TRANSCRIPT-selank-deep-dive|Selank deep dive]]

## Mechanisms
- `[educational]` Improves **phagocytosis**, educates **T-cells**, and shifts macrophages **M1 → M2** (pro- → anti-inflammatory) — see [[Cytokine Modulation]]. — [[TRANSCRIPT-selank-deep-dive|Selank deep dive]]

## Evidence
- `[educational]` Animal: improved sepsis survival, fungal-infection and autoimmune (MS/RA/lupus/IBD) models, some tumor inhibition. No human in-vivo data. — [[TRANSCRIPT-selank-deep-dive|Selank deep dive]]
