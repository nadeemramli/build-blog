## Evidence




- `[literature-animal]` Mimics pineal **epithalamin**; animal data: telomere lengthening, tumor suppression, circadian/melatonin recalibration, anti-inflammatory. Telomere↔longevity link **controversial**; no human trials — lifespan claims are a leap. — [[TRANSCRIPT-huberman-peptides-solo|Huberman]] 01:06–01:12

- `[literature-animal]` Mouse retinal-neuroprotection (retinitis pigmentosa, glaucoma) via **DNA-repair / genetic-machinery support**; Bakri hypothesises **melanopsin** upregulation (enhancing morning-light signalling). — [[TRANSCRIPT-huberman-bakri-peptides|Bakri]] 01:25–01:27

- `[educational]` Ranked **#2 longevity** — restores pineal melatonin/circadian + antioxidant + DNA/epigenetic stabilization + **telomere lengthening**. Preclinical lifespan: epitalon +11–16% flies, +12.3% max in mice, 6× ↓leukemia; epithalamin extract +11–31% mice / +25% rats; human data only for **epithalamin** (Russian mortality ↓1.6–2.5×). — [[TRANSCRIPT-top5-longevity-peptides|Longevity Top-5]]

## Anecdotes



- `[anecdote]` Russian bioregulator used for circadian rhythm; involved in DNA repair; explored in animal studies for retinal degeneration. `[note]` On the FDA do-not-compound list. — [[TRANSCRIPT-huberman-koniver-peptides|Koniver]] 01:27

- `[literature-russian]` Tripeptide **AEDG** from the **pineal**, mimics endogenous **epithalamin**; restores melatonin in *aged* (not young) animals; normalises clock genes + morning cortisol. Khavinson's **15-yr nursing-home study** (epithalamin + thymalin, ~10–20 days/yr) reported lower CVD/infection/cancer mortality. — [[TRANSCRIPT-huberman-bakri-peptides|Bakri]] 01:22–01:24

- `[anecdote-bb]` Community anti-aging dosing: **'Ukrainian' ~50 mg / 'Russian' ~100 mg every ~6 months** (telomerase rationale; effect unverifiable for years). Astragaloside IV cited as a cheaper telomerase-activation alternative. — [[TRANSCRIPT-anabolic-roundtable-peptides|Anabolic Roundtable]] ~01:30–01:36
