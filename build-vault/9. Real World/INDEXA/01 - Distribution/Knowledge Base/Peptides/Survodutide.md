## Evidence

- `[literature]` **First Phase 3 obesity readout — SYNCHRONIZE-1 (ADA 2026; NEJM + Nature Medicine):** 76-wk, 725 adults without T2D, **up to 16.6% weight loss** (≈17.8 kg) vs 3.2% placebo; MRI substudy fat-driven (visceral fat ↓34%, **liver fat ↓up to 63%**). Companion **SYNCHRONIZE-MASLD** supports liver-fat + weight reduction. Places survodutide in the dual-agonist tier below tirzepatide/retatrutide, with a standout liver/visceral-fat (glucagon-arm) signal. Not FDA-approved. — [[SOURCE-survodutide-synchronize1-phase3-2026]]
