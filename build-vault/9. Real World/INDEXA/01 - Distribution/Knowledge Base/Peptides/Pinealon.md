## Anecdotes


- `[anecdote]` Thought to regenerate pinealocytes; reported to roughly **double REM sleep** (tracked, Huberman n=1), with the effect persisting on non-dosing nights; Koniver reports the same pattern across patients; no negative side effects observed. — [[TRANSCRIPT-huberman-koniver-peptides|Koniver]] 01:24–01:30

- `[anecdote]` Tripeptide **EDR**; despite the name it derives from a **cortex** extract (cortexin), **not** the pineal. Epigenetic modifier (binds DNA groove); modulates PPARα/γ, SOD, IRIS → daytime alertness, HIIT performance, and more REM, with benefits persisting off-dose. — [[TRANSCRIPT-huberman-bakri-peptides|Bakri]] 01:08–01:18

## Physician protocols


- `[protocol]` Injectable, combined with glycine (oral glycine 3–5 g, up to 10 g, at bedtime). Can be pulsed intermittently rather than nightly. — [[TRANSCRIPT-huberman-koniver-peptides|Koniver]] 01:30–01:35

- `[protocol]` Oral ~0.5–3 mg (Russian Khavinson product ~200 mcg); some inject (systemic via PEPT transporters). Morning for cognition/performance; high evening doses sedate. — [[TRANSCRIPT-huberman-bakri-peptides|Bakri]] 01:13–01:16

## Practical notes


- `[warning]` Can **lower blood sugar / A1C** (PPAR activation — caution if hypoglycemia-prone) and cause **vivid dreams/nightmares**. — [[TRANSCRIPT-huberman-bakri-peptides|Bakri]] 01:17
