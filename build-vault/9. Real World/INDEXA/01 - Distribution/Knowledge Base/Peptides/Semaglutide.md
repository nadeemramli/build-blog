# Semaglutide

## Overview
- `[educational]` GLP-1 receptor agonist; an analog of endogenous **GLP-1** (a ~31-aa peptide). Native GLP-1 has a ~**2-min** half-life (cleaved by **DPP-4**); semaglutide is engineered (position-8 swap for DPP-4 resistance + a **position-26 fatty-acid chain** giving reversible **albumin binding**) for a **~7-day half-life**. Same molecule whether injected or oral. FDA-approved for type-2 diabetes and obesity. — [[TRANSCRIPT-oral-glp1-semaglutide-vs-orforglipron|Oral GLP-1 review]]

## Mechanisms
- `[educational]` Acts via [[GLP-1 Agonism]] across many tissues (pancreas, gut, hypothalamus, immune, kidney, endothelium, brain) — see that note. — [[TRANSCRIPT-oral-glp1-semaglutide-vs-orforglipron|Oral GLP-1 review]]

## Evidence

- `[educational]` **subQ bioavailability 89%** vs **oral 1–2%** (needs **SNAC** + strict fasted, ≤4 oz water, no food 30 min+ after). Weight loss: oral **25 mg → 13.6%**, **50 mg → 15.1%**, subQ **2.4 mg → 14.9%**, subQ **7.2 mg → 18.7%** (plateau above 7.2 mg subQ / 25–50 mg oral). A1C: oral 25/50 mg ≈ subQ (−1.8 to −2.0%). −14% MACE (no oral-vs-subQ difference). subQ has proven edge in CKD and fatty-liver disease. — [[TRANSCRIPT-oral-glp1-semaglutide-vs-orforglipron|Oral GLP-1 review]]

- `[literature]` Emerging **oncology** signal: GLP-1 RAs associated with reduced metastatic progression of obesity-related cancers (ASCO 2026) and better breast-cancer survival in obesity/T2D (JAMA Netw Open 2026, HR 0.35/0.09). Observational — not causal yet. — [[SOURCE-asco2026-glp1-cancer-progression]], [[SOURCE-glp1-breast-cancer-outcomes]]

- `[literature]` **Lower breast-cancer INCIDENCE (prevention axis; ASCO 2026 Abstract 10506, JCO Oncology Practice):** in 94,827 cancer-free women (BMI≥25), GLP-1 RA exposure → ~25–37% lower odds of developing breast cancer (matched OR 0.746, P<.0005); signal reportedly persisted after adjusting for weight loss. Observational. — [[SOURCE-glp1-breast-cancer-incidence-mcdonald-2026]]

- `[literature]` **Alzheimer's — NEGATIVE Phase 3 (EVOKE / EVOKE+, Lancet 2026):** in 3,808 amyloid-positive adults with early AD, **oral semaglutide 14 mg missed the CDR-SB primary endpoint at 104 wk** (no cognitive or functional benefit vs placebo) despite a ~30% CRP drop. The definitive interventional test of the observational "−45% dementia-risk" claim — and it **did not replicate as a treatment effect**. — [[SOURCE-semaglutide-evoke-alzheimers-phase3-2026]]

- `[literature]` **Amylin/GLP-1 combo (CagriSema, REDEFINE-1/-2, ADA 2025 / NEJM):** semaglutide 2.4 mg + cagrilintide 2.4 mg → **20.4% weight loss** (68 wk, no-diabetes; 22.7% full-adherence) vs 14.9% for semaglutide alone — the amylin arm adds meaningfully on top of GLP-1. NDA filed; FDA decision expected late 2026. — [[SOURCE-cagrisema-redefine-phase3-2026]]

## Anecdotes


- `[educational]` Ranked **#1 longevity** (GLP-1 class) — meta-analyses (100k pts): **−12% all-cause death**; obesity-no-diabetes −51–77%. Beyond weight: anti-inflammatory, **−45% dementia risk**, ↑mito/↓oxidative, ↓senescence/↑autophagy. Animal: low-dose from mid-life gave rapamycin-like aging benefits without weight change → low-dose from late-30s+ may aid longevity (human data pending). — [[TRANSCRIPT-top5-longevity-peptides|Longevity Top-5]]

## Physician protocols
- `[protocol]` **Start low, titrate slow** (limits GI side effects, preserves muscle). Oral must be taken overnight-fasted with ≤4 oz water, no food 30 min+ after, swallowed whole. ~56% discontinue oral within 1 year (regimen burden). — [[TRANSCRIPT-oral-glp1-semaglutide-vs-orforglipron|Oral GLP-1 review]]

## Practical notes
- `[educational]` High oral absorption variability (up to ~137% day-to-day in one person; ~56% between people) — non-response may be absorption, not dose. — [[TRANSCRIPT-oral-glp1-semaglutide-vs-orforglipron|Oral GLP-1 review]]
