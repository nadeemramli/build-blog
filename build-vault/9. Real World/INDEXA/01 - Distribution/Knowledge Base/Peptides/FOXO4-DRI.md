# FOXO4-DRI

## Overview
- `[educational]` A synthetic **senolytic** peptide — a **D-retro-inverso** modification of the FOXO4 sequence (reversed order, D- instead of L-amino acids) plus an added peptide segment. Designed to clear **senescent ("zombie") cells**, a hallmark of aging. — [[TRANSCRIPT-top5-longevity-peptides|Longevity Top-5]]

## Mechanisms
- `[educational]` [[Senolysis]]: blocks natural FOXO4 from sequestering **p53** in the nucleus of senescent cells → p53 exits → triggers **apoptosis** of the senescent cell (sparing healthy cells). — [[TRANSCRIPT-top5-longevity-peptides|Longevity Top-5]]

## Evidence
- `[educational]` Mouse: ↓testicular senescence (↑testosterone, ↑sperm quality), ↓pulmonary-fibrosis senescence, cardiac-aging (related blocker), and in aged-healthy mice → ↑fur density, renal function, fitness (healthspan). **Human: only one in-vitro study** (clearing senescent cartilage cells); **no in-vivo human data** → ranked #4 of the longevity top-5 on mechanism strength despite thin evidence. — [[TRANSCRIPT-top5-longevity-peptides|Longevity Top-5]]
