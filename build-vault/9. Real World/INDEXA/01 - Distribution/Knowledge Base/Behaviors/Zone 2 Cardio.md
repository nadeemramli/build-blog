# Zone 2 Cardio

## What it is
- Sustained **low-intensity aerobic** work at the top of fat-oxidation zone (conversational pace; ~60–70% max HR) — includes the **Zone 2 walk**.

## How it works
- Drives **mitochondrial biogenesis** and fat-oxidation capacity (the same [[Mitochondrial Bioenergetics]] axis [[MOTS-C]] targets — they're synergistic).

## Goal relevance
- Foundational metabolic-health/longevity work and fat loss; pairs with mitochondrial peptides.

## How to do it
- ~**150–180+ min/week** across sessions of 30–60 min.

## Cautions
- Low-risk; build volume gradually.
