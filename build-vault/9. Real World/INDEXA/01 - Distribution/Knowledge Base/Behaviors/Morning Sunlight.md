# Morning Sunlight

## What it is
- Getting **bright outdoor light in the eyes early after waking** (and full-spectrum daytime light).

## How it works
- Anchors the **circadian rhythm** (cortisol-awakening response, evening melatonin timing) via melanopsin signalling — the same pathway [[Epithalon]] is hypothesised to support (melanopsin upregulation).

## Goal relevance
- Foundational for **sleep/REM** and for the nighttime GH pulse that pre-sleep GH peptides augment; supports hormonal health broadly.

## How to do it
- ~10–30 min outdoor light soon after waking; pair with darkness/low blue light at night.

## Cautions
- Don't stare at the sun; UV caution midday.
