# Contrast Therapy

## What it is
- Alternating **hot ([[Sauna]]) and cold ([[Cold Plunge]])** exposure in cycles.

## How it works
- Repeated vasoconstriction/vasodilation acts as a vascular 'pump' → circulation, perceived recovery, and combined hormetic signalling.

## Goal relevance
- Recovery and circulation; complements repair-phase healing.

## How to do it
- Several hot↔cold cycles, typically ending on cold (alerting) or hot (relaxation) depending on goal/time of day.

## Cautions
- Same as sauna + cold plunge individually.
