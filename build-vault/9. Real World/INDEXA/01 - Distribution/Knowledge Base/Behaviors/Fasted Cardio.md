# Fasted Cardio

## What it is
- Aerobic work (often **fasted [[Zone 2 Cardio]]** or fasted endurance) done in an overnight-fasted state.

## How it works
- Low insulin + low glycogen → greater reliance on **fat oxidation**; the fasted state also keeps somatostatin low and GH higher (why GH-secretagogue / fat-loss peptides are often timed pre-fasted-cardio).

## Goal relevance
- Fat-loss protocols; a prime window to time **morning peptides** (e.g. [[MOTS-C]] 30 min prior, or a morning GH-secretagogue dose).

## How to do it
- Morning, fasted, low-to-moderate intensity (high-intensity fasted can be catabolic).

## Cautions
- Don't do heavy/HIIT fasted if it costs performance or lean mass.
