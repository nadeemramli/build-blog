# HIIT

## What it is
- **High-Intensity Interval Training** — short maximal/near-maximal efforts with rest.

## How it works
- Potent stimulus for an exercise-induced **GH** pulse, EPOC, and mitochondrial/metabolic adaptation.

## Goal relevance
- Time-efficient fat loss + fitness; an exercise-driven GH pulse can be a daytime peptide-timing anchor.

## How to do it
- 1–3×/week, kept distinct from heavy resistance sessions for recovery.

## Cautions
- High systemic stress — manage recovery; not ideal fasted.
