# Fasting (Time-Restricted Eating)

## What it is
- Caloric restriction windows — from daily **time-restricted eating** to longer fasts.

## How it works
- Low insulin/glucose → **AMPK** activation, raised endogenous **GH** (somatostatin low), increased fat oxidation, autophagy, and a [[Mitohormesis|hormetic]] adaptation that **MOTS-C partly mimics** ('exercise/fasting in a vial').

## Goal relevance
- Fat loss + metabolic/longevity; explains why pre-sleep and morning peptide dosing is timed away from food (food blunts the GH response).

## How to do it
- e.g. a daily eating window; avoid eating ~2–4 h before a pre-sleep GH peptide.

## Cautions
- Can erode lean mass / training performance if too aggressive; not for everyone.
