# Cold Plunge

## What it is
- **Cold-water immersion** (deliberate cold exposure), typically ~3–8°C for short durations.

## How it works
- Sympathetic activation → noradrenaline/dopamine spike, brown-fat/thermogenesis, vasoconstriction→rebound vasodilation; a hormetic stressor ([[Mitohormesis]]).

## Goal relevance
- Mood/alertness, metabolic (cold-induced thermogenesis), recovery. **Note:** avoid in the **4–8 h after resistance training** if hypertrophy/strength is the goal (cold blunts the inflammatory adaptation signal).

## How to do it
- A few minutes, a few times per week; earlier in the day preferred (alerting).

## Cautions
- Cardiac risk with extreme cold; never alone in open water.
