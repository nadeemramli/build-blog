# Sleep Hygiene

## What it is
- Behavioral practices that protect sleep quantity/quality and circadian alignment.

## How it works
- Cool, dark room; consistent schedule; [[Morning Sunlight]] + evening low-light; no late heavy meals/alcohol; caffeine cutoff. Deep sleep onset is when the **largest GH pulse** occurs.

## Goal relevance
- The substrate every other protocol depends on — and the timing anchor for pre-sleep peptides ([[Sermorelin]]/[[Ipamorelin]], [[Pinealon]]). The foundation of the **sleep/REM** and **muscle/recovery** stacks.

## How to do it
- Fixed wake time; ~18–20°C room; screens/blue light down at night; alcohol erodes REM.

## Cautions
- Alcohol and late food both blunt REM and the GH pulse.
