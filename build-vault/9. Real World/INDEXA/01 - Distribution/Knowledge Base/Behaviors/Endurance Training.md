# Endurance Training

## What it is
- Longer aerobic/cardiorespiratory work above [[Zone 2 Cardio]] duration/intensity (incl. **fasted endurance**).

## How it works
- Broad cardiovascular + mitochondrial adaptations; aerobic work **above the lactate threshold for ≥10 min** can also trigger an exercise-induced **GH** pulse.

## Goal relevance
- Cardiorespiratory fitness/longevity; [[MOTS-C]] preferentially benefits endurance (slow-twitch) capacity and is synergistic with (not a replacement for) endurance work.

## How to do it
- Progressive volume; mix with Zone 2 base.

## Cautions
- Overtraining/under-recovery if stacked with aggressive caloric deficit.
