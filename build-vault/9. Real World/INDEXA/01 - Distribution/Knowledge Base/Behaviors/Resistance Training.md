# Resistance Training

## What it is
- Strength/hypertrophy training with progressive overload.

## How it works
- Drives an exercise-induced **GH/IGF-1** response (best with large compound lifts, moderate-heavy loads, short rests) and builds the **muscle mass** that strongly predicts longevity.

## Goal relevance
- The foundation of muscle/recovery and fat-loss protocols; **preserves lean mass** during GLP-1 weight loss; amplifies GH-peptide benefit.

## How to do it
- 2–4×/week; adequate protein (~2 g/kg/day) supports it and helps lower SHBG.

## Cautions
- Avoid cold plunge in the 4–8 h after if hypertrophy is the goal.
