An effective marketing angle often zeroes in on a key benefit, emotion, or problem that your audience cares about. For example, instead of just selling a vacuum cleaner as a cleaning tool, the angle might be "effortless home perfection for busy parents" — it’s specific, relatable, and taps into a real need.
